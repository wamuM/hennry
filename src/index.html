Vocabulary

Egg: An oval or round object laid by a female bird, reptile, fish, or invertebrate, usually containing a developing embryo. The eggs of birds are enclosed in a chalky shell, while those of reptiles are in a leathery membrane.
Keel: A ridge along the breastbone of many birds to which the flight muscles are attached; the carina.
Hen: A female bird, especially of a domestic fowl.
Chick: A young bird, especially one newly hatched.
Peck: A stroke or bite by a bird with its beak.
Chicken: A domestic fowl kept for its eggs or meat, especially a young one.
Wattle: A fleshy wrinkled and often brightly colored fold of skin hanging from the neck or throat of certain birds (chickens and turkeys) or lizards.
Chook: A chicken, especially a hen.
Preen: Tidy and clean its feathers with its beak.
Poultry: Domestic fowl, such as chickens, turkeys, ducks, and geese.
Hock: The joint in a quadruped's hind leg between the knee and the fetlock, the angle of which points backwards.
Flock: A number of birds of one kind feeding, resting, or travelling together.
Roo: Sland for a rooster or cock
Spurs: Appendages towards the back of a rooster's shanks that are used for defensive fightind and flock protection.

Controls

Space Bar to skip dialogue.
Left Click to move to clicked position(keep pressed to dash)
Right Click to eat (keep pressed)
M to mute/unmute the game
D to enable devtools(disables score mode)
L to go to the next level
X to delete all save data

How to play

Try to dodge the other chickens and eat special seeds to gain powers
Green Seeds More life(WIP)
Blue Seeds Shield (WIP)
